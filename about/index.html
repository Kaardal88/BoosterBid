About BoosterBid

Who We Are

BoosterBid was founded in 2020 by a small team passionate about online auctions. Today, we’ve grown into a global auction house with dedicated employees committed to making buying and selling easy, safe, and exciting. We offer worldwide shipping, fast customer support, and a secure system for both buyers and sellers. Our core values are fairness, transparency, and user-friendliness.

Rules

  • You must always bid at least 1 credit higher than the current bid.
  • All bids are binding and cannot be withdrawn.
  • Auction winners must complete the purchase within 48 hours.
  • Worldwide shipping is available – shipping costs are covered by the buyer unless stated otherwise.
  • Only registered and verified accounts can place bids.
